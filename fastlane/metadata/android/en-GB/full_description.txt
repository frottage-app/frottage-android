This is an art project by friends, shared with fellow enthusiasts.

It only does one thing well: give you groovy wallpapers every day.

*   No fuss, no ads, just a minimal and simple app.
*   Easily set wallpapers manually or schedule daily updates.
*   Save your favorite images.
